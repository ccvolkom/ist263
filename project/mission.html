Cut The Cookie's Mission

Cut The Cookie was started as a way for avid bakers to find the best recipes in the easiest way possible. We compile the best of the best and test each recipe for quality assurance. We are constantly sorting through new recipes and techniques to give to our readers. The website is a haven for all bakers and a community of baked good lovers. We also decided to allow readers to submit feedback so that we can continue to improve our recipes and process.

Our goal is foster better bakers! Join the community and make some of the best cookies you’ve ever had.

If you are interested in submitting a recipe or have comments about one that’s posted, please visit our contact page and send us an email.
